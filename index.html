About FloodShield Jigawa

FloodShield Jigawa is a startup building a smart early warning system that uses satelite Imagery, AI, and smart sensors to predict and alert Flood threats in real-time, especially for vulnerable rural communities in Jigawa State and beyond

Platform Features

Satelite Data

Detects flood-prone zones from space in real-time

AI-Powered Alerts

Process Rainfall and surface water levels to sends smart alerts

Multi-channel Warning

Alerts Via SMS, Mobile app, Sound alarms, and web dashboard
